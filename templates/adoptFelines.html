Hi, my name is Briggs!

This frisky feline is the epitome of what is to be a cat. Briggs makes you work for the pets of his soft glorious fur. He has mastered the art of "playing hard to get" with his come hither looks but don't mess his hair up because he has it just the way he likes it. Some petting is allowed on his terms but too many or too little will require a toll or savory temptations treats. Long play times with his favorite wand toys of feathered jingly glory are a MUST!

A perch with a window view is a requirement for his extra curricular activities of judgmental creature watching. A hide-a-way bed would be a lovely addition for a retreat away from the harsh stresses of his grooming regimen. As an only child Briggs isn't too sure about the intruders called "dogs" just yet, who reside in his foster home. He prefers to be an only cat in his humble abode as he has not yet learned how to share. Do you have the perseverance and determination to win the heart and friendship of this dashing young man?!
